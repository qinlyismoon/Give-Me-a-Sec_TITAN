Behind the Disinformation

Interaction

Hovering over pixelated “information” or “disinformation” causes the text to disintegrate into particles, gradually revealing the word TITAN beneath “disinformation”.

Description

Within a grid of repetitive “information,” certain entries subtly distort—signaling disinformation. When interacted with, these unstable words break apart into granular particles, gradually exposing TITAN beneath the noise.

Design Intention

This piece introduces TITAN as a conceptual presence embedded within the visual field. TITAN beneath fragmented surface layers illustrates how AI-supported inquiry operates beneath the illusion of clarity—revealing through interaction.
