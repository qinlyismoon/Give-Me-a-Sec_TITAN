Type to Know TITAN

Interaction

Typing the letters T–I–T–A–N sequentially unlocks parts of an alpha-masked image. Once fully typed, users can align the revealed image with background content, clarifying fragmented visual information.

Description

This interactive piece transforms the act of typing into a mechanism of visual unmasking. Each correct keystroke reveals a portion of a hidden image, masked through transparency. Only when the full word “TITAN” is entered does the image fully resolve—allowing the user to align fragmented layers and see more clearly what was once obscured.

Design Intention

As a conversational AI, TITAN begins with language—and through that language, it helps users practice critical thinking and gain clarity. Typing “TITAN” becomes a symbolic entry point: to name the tool is to activate its potential, to bring coherence to complexity, and to see through the noise of information.
