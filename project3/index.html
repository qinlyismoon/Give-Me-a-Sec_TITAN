TITAN Is...

Interaction

Upon clicking, three core ideas—Socratic Method, Experiential Learning, and Conversational AI—gradually appear in randomized order. Each is revealed through pixelated typography, subtle color shifts, and auditory cues, inviting the user into a layered discovery of TITAN’s conceptual structure.

Description

This interactive piece introduces the foundational identity of TITAN, an AI-powered critical thinking assistant. The experience requires the user to engage, reveal, and assemble meaning from fragments. Each concept emerges independently, emphasizing their role in shaping TITAN’s function: as a scaffold for inquiry.

Design Intention

titan is… functions as an interface-as-diagram, visually and temporally unpacking the philosophical and pedagogical logic behind the TITAN system. This slow, conceptual emergence reflects TITAN’s mission: to support active critical thinking.
