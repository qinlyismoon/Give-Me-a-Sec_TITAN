Give Me a Sec

A Socratic Pixelated Journey through Critical Thinking and Disinformation

Inspired by TITAN, a conversational AI rooted in the Socratic method, this collection of interactive experiences explores what critical thinking feels like, looks like, and sounds like.

Each piece invites you to pause, probe, and participate—to reflect on how you perceive information, resist superficial clarity, and re-engage with the slow, intentional labor of making sense.

Across gestures, sound, and text, these works ask not for immediate answers, but for a second—a moment to think with, through, and beyond the interface.
