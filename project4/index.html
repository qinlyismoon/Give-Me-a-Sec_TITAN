Voicing Forward

Interaction

Voice volume controls a white ball that navigates a horizontal path blocked by vertical pillars labeled with Socratic questions. As the user speaks, the ball rises or falls to pass through each barrier. New prompts are revealed sequentially.

Description

This interactive system transforms the Socratic method into a sound-controlled, spatial experience. Users progress by modulating their voice volume, guiding a ball through a sequence of labeled gates: author?, professional?, purpose?, bias?, stereotypes?. Each cleared pillar reveals the next, forming a rhythm of inquiry that mirrors the structure of critical questioning.

Design Intention

"Voicing Forward" operationalizes critical thinking as embodied learning. Voice becomes action. Movement becomes method. The interface rejects passive reception, requiring users to speak their way forward. Titan is positioned as a co-participant, supporting epistemic progression.
